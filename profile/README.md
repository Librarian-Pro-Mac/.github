## Overview

Librarian Pro Advanced is a comprehensive personal collection management application that catalogs books, movies, music, games, software, and any other media through barcode scanning and automatic online metadata retrieval, turning any personal collection into a searchable, organized inventory.

Barcode scanning via webcam or external scanner retrieves full item details from Amazon and other online databases automatically, filling in title, author, description, cover art, and other metadata without manual data entry for each item.

Smart collections organize items automatically based on criteria you define, creating dynamic views of your library that surface specific genres, ratings, formats, or any other attribute without requiring manual folder management.

Lending tracking monitors which items have been loaned to which friends, with due date reminders and contact information that make sure borrowed items are remembered and returned without uncomfortable follow-up conversations.

For collectors, book lovers, movie enthusiasts, and anyone who has ever struggled to remember what they own or to whom they have lent something, Librarian Pro Advanced is the inventory tool that brings order to any personal collection without overwhelming complexity.

## Performance

Librarian Pro balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management reduces overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance compared to software-only solutions. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
